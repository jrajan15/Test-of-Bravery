Welcome to the Test of Bravery!

HOW TO PLAY:

Test of Bravery is a dice rolling simulation game where you click on the dice icon to roll. The number that you roll will be added to your total score for that round. The more you roll, it continues to add to your score, but be thoughtful when you choose to stop, because if at any point you roll a 1, your score is 0. If you feel satisfied with the score that you have, you can end the round safely by clicking on the piggy bank to bank your score. When you bank, you are done for that round, but you can save yourself from a possible 0 if you continued to roll. Your score for all 5 rounds will be totaled in the final score. All the best, and be thoughtful and careful on your roll.
